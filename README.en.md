## Hi, I'm Felipe 👋

I'm a **Growth Product Manager**: I find where a product's growth is stuck and unblock it with data, experiments and focus.

With one difference: I don't stop at the roadmap. When a hypothesis needs software to be validated, **I build it myself** — these repos are the proof.

- 📈 **Growth with method** — hypothesis, experiment, metric. No vanity metrics.
- 📊 **Data to decisions** — executive boards, Gantt and PERT views that read in 30 seconds.
- 🛠️ **A PM who builds** — I prototype, validate and ship without waiting for anyone's sprint.
- 🌅 **With identity** — a product can be useful *and* have a soul. Ask the capybara below.

## 🧭 The journey, in four acts

1. **Automate** — Excel, SQL and Python scripts that gave hours back: I learned growth starts by removing friction.
2. **Build** — SPAs and sites in Vue and JavaScript, to understand product from the code up.
3. **End-to-end product** — AgileFlow, FlowBoard and Pomodoro Llanero: I spotted the problem, scoped it and shipped it.
4. **Growth** — today I combine [data, product and execution](https://github.com/castellanosfelipe) to grow whatever I touch — documented like a product brief.
